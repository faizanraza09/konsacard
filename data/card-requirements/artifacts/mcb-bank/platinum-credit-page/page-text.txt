With our perfect combination of premier features and superior services, the enclosed rewards and recognition offers you the prestige and luxury of a lifestyle you can uniquely call your own.
MCB Visa Platinum Credit Card is the most reliable, competitive and gratifying Credit Card offering a tantalising mix of luxurious shopping, dining and travelling opportunities, which will undoubtedly be a treat for you and your family.
It is accepted at nearly 29 million locations in more than 150 countries around the globe and over 30,000 establishments in Pakistan. So make the most of a powerful card and expect what you deserve, a liberating experience that redefines the way you spend.

MCB Lounge Access
MCB VISA Credit Cardholders, including Visa Platinum members, you are privileged to enjoy complimentary access to airport lounges globally, enhancing your travel experience across Central Europe, the Middle East, and Africa (CEMEA).
Experience all-in-one digital travel with VISA AIRPORT CAMPANION powered by DragonPass – a renowned global leader in lifestyle and travel services. With a reach spanning over 1,200 lounges across 140+ countries, we cater to the needs of million travelers worldwide.
The Visa Airport Companion (VAC) offers a seamless and enriching travel experience tailored just for cardholders:
For lounge access within Pakistan, a nominal fee of PKR 1, will be charged per visit using your MCB Visa Platinum Credit Card
For international lounge access, a cumulative international transaction value of USD 5 is required within 90 days prior to availing the service
A maximum of 06 complimentary visits per calendar year is allowed
Any visits beyond the entitled limit or outside the Visa lounge program will be charged directly to the cardholder via the Visa Companion App
Lounge access and stay are subject to the terms, conditions, and policies of the respective lounge operators and may vary by location
How to Get Started with MCB Lounge
Sign up is a simple process, following the below steps.
Step 1: Eligible Visa cardholders must download the Visa Airport Companion (VAC) app, available at no cost from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
Step 2: Once downloaded, select Sign Up and complete the information as requested including:
Eligible Visa card details:
Card number
Expiry date
CVV number
Country of issuance
Name, email address, and mobile number
Consent to Terms and Conditions, Privacy Policy, and Terms of Use.
Step 3: A six-digit code will be sent to the cardholder’s mobile number or email address for verification.
Step 4: After successful verification, the cardholder can create a password and will be directed to the app homepage where their benefits can be accessed straight away!
Note: Minimum transaction value of USD 1 at Point-of-Sale transactions including Face to Face, E-Commerce, MOTO or Recurring transactions where Issuer is not located in the country of transaction.
