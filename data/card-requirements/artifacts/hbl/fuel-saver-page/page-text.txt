Overview
HBL FuelSaver CreditCard offers an exciting saving package by reducing the burden of monthly fuel expense.
Receive up to 5% cashback* on transactions on fuel and reduce the burden of your monthly expenses by saving up to Rs. 2,000/-* every month. There is no fee and you will automatically be enrolled in the Fuel Savings Program. The Fuel Savings Program is only available on HBL FuelSaver Cards and is not offered on other HBL CreditCards.
To apply for a card, please call HBL PhoneBanking at 111-111-425
*Terms and Conditions Apply

Card Management on HBL Mobile
Manage all your credit card needs with just a few taps on HBL Mobile. Use HBL Mobile to:
Activate your HBL CreditCard and generate PIN
Enable / disable card usage at ecommerce, POS machines, ATM and contactless transactions
View transactions and the monthly statement for your credit card
Pay your bill for HBL or any other bank’s credit card
Block your HBL CreditCard
Apply for a supplementary credit card
Make utility bill payments and mobile top-ups
View your card details on the go
Apply for credit limit enhancement
Get your advance tax certificate
Instantly access exciting deals & discounts, installment plans and products available on HBL Rewards Program
Manage international transactions by enabling/disabling specific countries
Start managing your HBL CreditCard today!
*These services are currently available to basic cardholders registered on HBL Mobile
