Spend Duration:
Spend within 60 days of statement generation date in which annual fee is levied
Requests for annual fee reversals will be entertained up to 120 days from the statement generation date in which annual fee is levied
Annual fee is charged at the time of card activation and annually on the anniversary date. The FED against Annual fee will not be reversed
