Show notesHide notes
  • Shared public eligibility grid across Classic, Gold, and Platinum.
  • The lowest published salaried threshold on the official page is PKR 35,000 for GOP/Semi-Govt./Autonomous permanent employees; approved-company and non-approved-company applicants are shown at higher salary bands.
  • SEB/SEP applicants are shown separately on the official page with PKR 150,000 average-balance criterion.
  • The FAQ on the same page splits maximum age as 60 for permanent salaried, 65 for contractual salaried/pensioners, and 70 for SEB/SEP.
  • Debit-card pages do not clearly publish age criteria.
  • Debit cards are mostly account-linked, so salary requirements are usually absent.
  • Some shared credit-card eligibility values need later refinement by applicant category rather than one single threshold.
