Eligibility & fees

Key requirements and fees for this card. Always verify current terms directly with the bank.

Annual feeFree
Fee waiver: No annual or issuance fee while Premier Infinite criteria are maintained; customers not meeting the Premier Infinite AUM criteria are charged USD 500 equivalent in PKR.
Data confidence: medium
Show notesHide notes
  • Premier Infinite is presented publicly as an invitation-only Premier product rather than a standard income-gated card.
  • Official Premier SOC states no annual and issuance fee for Premier Visa Infinite Credit Card, but customers not meeting the Premier Infinite AUM criteria are charged USD 500 equivalent in PKR.
  • The Islamic SOC mentions a PKR 150 Million AUM threshold for 'Islamic Infinite Banking' — this appears to be a distinct ultra-premium tier, not the conventional Premier Infinite tier.
  • The conventional Premier Infinite AUM threshold is not published clearly in accessible SOC documents. The conventional Premier Infinite Debit Card uses PKR 1.5M account balance; the credit card threshold is likely higher but unconfirmed. Needs manual verification against a current conventional SOC.
  • Several Premier / Infinite family cards still need cleaner separation between conventional, Premier, and Islamic Premier records.
  • Debit-card age requirements were generally not stated.
  • Some Islamic debit-card fee values were recovered from older surfaced SOC material rather than a current dedicated card page.
