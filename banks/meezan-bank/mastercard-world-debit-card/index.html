Show notesHide notes
  • Official Jan-Jun 2026 SOC lists Mastercard World issuance and annual fee at PKR 27,500 each.
  • World Debit Card is part of Meezan Premium Banking. PKR 5,000,000 in accounts or PKR 7,000,000 in Term Deposit Certificates required. No annual fee for qualifying customers.
  • There are two competing eligibility frames: premium-customer thresholds on the card page and a lower PKR 500,000 issuance or average-balance threshold in the SOC PDF.
  • minimum_account_balance_pkr is anchored to the lower explicit bank-account threshold (PKR 500,000) while the higher relationship-balance criterion is preserved separately.
  • No public Meezan consumer credit-card catalogue was found in this pass.
  • PayPak fees conflict between the card page and the 2026 SOC PDF.
  • Most ordinary debit-card pages do not publish salary or age requirements.
