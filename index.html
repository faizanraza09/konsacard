How Foodcard Works

foodcard.pk compares dining offers on Pakistani bank cards by city, restaurant, bank, card type, day, and bill size. Instead of showing a flat list, it estimates which card is likely to save you the most for the way you actually dine.

Cities Covered

The current comparison covers dining deals in Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad. You can filter offers by city to focus on restaurant discounts that are relevant to your location instead of scanning generic bank offer pages.

Banks and Restaurant Deals

foodcard.pk brings together bank card dining deals from major Pakistani banks and compares them against restaurant offers in one place. This makes it easier to see which card works best for cafes, grills, buffets, pizza chains, and everyday dining.

The homepage compares offers from banks such as HBL, Bank Alfalah, Askari Bank, Meezan Bank, UBL, Allied Bank, Bank AL Habib, Faysal Bank, BankIslami, JS Bank, Habib Metro, MCB, MCB Islamic, and Bank of Punjab.

How We Rank Cards

Cards are ranked by estimated savings on your bill, restaurant coverage, and how well the offer days match your selected going-out days.
