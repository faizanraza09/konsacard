How we source the data

konsacard.pk combines public offer listings, official campaign pages, and merchant partner pages into one comparison layer. We standardize city names, discount percentages, card labels, and caps where the source publishes them.

  • We prioritize official bank or first-party partner pages when available.
  • Some sources publish exact caps and exclusions, while others only publish headline discounts.
  • Cards are ranked by the data available in the source, not by paid placement.
