Privacy Policy

This page explains what konsacard.pk collects, what it does not collect, and how analytics and advertising tags are used on the site.

What this site collects

konsacard.pk does not ask users to create accounts, submit restaurant lists to a backend, or upload personal files. Most site interactions happen locally in the browser using the public offers dataset that loads with the page.

Basic usage analytics may be collected through Google Analytics to understand visits, traffic sources, and general site performance. Advertising scripts may also set or read cookies if Google AdSense begins serving ads on the site.

How the data is used

  • To understand which pages and guides are being used.
  • To improve site performance, layout, and navigation.
  • To support advertising eligibility and ad delivery if ads are enabled.

What this site does not do

  • It does not sell user accounts or subscription data because there are no user accounts.
  • It does not ask for CNIC, salary, card numbers, or banking credentials.
  • It does not process payments.

Third-party services

konsacard.pk may use Google Analytics and Google AdSense. Those services can collect technical usage information according to Google’s own policies.

If you want to contact the site owner about privacy questions or correction requests, use the contact details on the contact page.
