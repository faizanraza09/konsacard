Privacy Policy and Disclaimers

This page explains what konsacard.pk collects, what it does not, how the AI chat feature handles your queries, and the legal terms that apply when you use this site. By using konsacard.pk you agree to these terms.

What this site does not collect

konsacard.pk has no user accounts, no login, and no backend that stores your comparisons. When you use the tool by selecting restaurants, adjusting bill size, or filtering cards, all of that runs locally in your browser against the public offers dataset that loads with the page. Nothing is sent to a server.

  • No name, email address, or CNIC collected (unless you choose to email us).
  • No salary, account balance, or financial data stored on any server. Inputs stay in browser memory only and disappear when you close the tab.
  • No card numbers, banking credentials, or payment information.
  • No payments processed on this site.
  • Wallet selections and filter preferences are saved locally in your own browser via the standard browser storage (localStorage) so they survive a refresh. That data stays on your device and is not visible to us.

The AI chat feature (KonsaCard AI)

The "Ask AI" feature in the tool is powered by Google's Gemini API. When you send a message in the chat:

  • What is sent. Your message text, the current filter state (city, card types, bill size), and the top ranked cards from your current search. No personal information is included unless you type it yourself.
  • Where it goes. Directly to Google's Gemini API over HTTPS. The query is processed by Google's AI infrastructure according to Google's Gemini API Terms of Service.
  • What is stored. Chat history lives in browser memory only for the duration of your session. It is not stored on konsacard.pk servers. Clearing the chat or closing the tab clears the history.
  • How to opt out. Simply don't use the chat feature. The comparison tool works fully without it.
Note: Please do not type sensitive personal information into the chat (such as salary figures, CNIC numbers, or account details). The AI does not need any of this to give useful recommendations, and the message goes straight to Google's servers.

Google's data collection practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. If you want to opt out of Google Analytics tracking, the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on is the standard mechanism.

How our offers data is sourced

The discount offers shown on konsacard.pk are aggregated from public sources. These include each bank's published offer pages, publicly accessible merchant feeds (for example the Peekaboo platform that several Pakistani banks use to publish dining offers, and the Easypaisa Discount World programme), and merchant workbooks published by the issuing banks themselves (such as the NBP merchant spreadsheet). We do not have a commercial relationship with any of these sources.

Bank names and card names are used nominatively to identify the products being compared. Bank logos and trademarks remain the property of their respective owners and are used solely for the purpose of identification. konsacard.pk is independent and is not sponsored, endorsed by, or affiliated with any bank, payment scheme, or restaurant.
