Privacy Policy

What konsacard.pk collects, what it doesn't, and how the AI chat feature handles your data.

What this site does not collect

konsacard.pk has no user accounts, no login, and no backend that stores your comparisons. When you use the tool — selecting restaurants, adjusting bill size, filtering cards — all of that runs locally in your browser against the public offers dataset that loads with the page. Nothing is sent to a server.

  • No name, email address, or CNIC collected (unless you choose to email us)
  • No salary, account balance, or financial data stored anywhere; inputs stay in browser memory only and disappear when you close the tab
  • No card numbers, banking credentials, or payment information
  • No user accounts or persistent profiles
  • No payments processed on this site

The AI chat feature (KonsaCard AI)

The "Ask AI" feature in the tool is powered by Google's Gemini API. When you send a message in the chat:

  • What is sent: Your message text, the current filter state (city, card types, bill size), and the top ranked cards from your current search. No personal information is included unless you type it yourself.
  • Where it goes: Directly to Google's Gemini API over HTTPS. The query is processed by Google's AI infrastructure according to Google's Gemini API Terms of Service.
  • What is stored: Chat history exists in browser memory only for the duration of your session. It is not stored on konsacard.pk servers. Clearing the chat or closing the tab clears the history.
  • How to opt out: Simply don't use the chat feature. The comparison tool works fully without it.
Note: Don't type sensitive personal information into the chat: salary figures, CNIC numbers, account details. The AI doesn't need any of this to give useful recommendations, and it goes straight to Google's servers.

Google Analytics and AdSense

Both scripts are loaded on page view. Google Analytics collects usage data to help understand site traffic and performance. Google AdSense may serve ads and set cookies in line with Google's own advertising policies.

Google's data collection practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. If you want to opt out of Google Analytics tracking, the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on is the standard mechanism.

External links

The tool links to bank application pages and offer pages. konsacard.pk has no control over and takes no responsibility for the privacy practices of those external sites. When you follow a link to a bank's website, their privacy policy applies from that point forward.
