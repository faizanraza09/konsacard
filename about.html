About

Why konsacard.pk exists

konsacard.pk was built to answer a simple but surprisingly hard question in Pakistan: which restaurant discount card is actually worth having for the places you really go.

The problem

Dining offers are fragmented across bank pages, merchant promotions, and partner listings. That makes it easy to see a single deal and hard to compare the whole picture across cities, days, restaurants, and real bill sizes.

The goal

This site tries to make that comparison practical. Instead of asking where one existing card works, it is built around a broader consumer question: which card should someone actually choose for how they dine.

What the site is not

  • It is not a bank.
  • It is not a card application portal.
  • It is not a sponsored ranking board.
